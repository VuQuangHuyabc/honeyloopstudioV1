Shipping Policy

Enjoy fast shipping with a 1-day preparation time and delivery in 2–3 days.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1 business day. Orders placed before 2 PM EST will be processed the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM EST will be processed the next business day.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Times

Shipping Method Cost Delivery Time
Standard Shipping $5.99 2-3 business days
Express Shipping $12.99 1-2 business days
Free Shipping FREE 2-3 business days
Free Shipping: Orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping.

Shipping Destinations

Honey Loop Studio LLC currently ships to all addresses within the United States, including Alaska and Hawaii. We do not currently offer international shipping.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number on our website or the carrier's website.

Shipping Confirmation

You will receive a shipping confirmation email once your order has been dispatched. This email will include your tracking number and estimated delivery date.

Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with your delivery, please contact us immediately at contact@honeyloopstudio.com. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve any problems and ensure you receive your order.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will file a claim with the shipping carrier and arrange for a replacement or refund.

Address Changes

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact us within 2 hours of placing your order. We cannot guarantee address changes after this time as your order may already be processed.

Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver all orders on time, please note that shipping delays may occur during peak seasons, holidays, or due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions or carrier issues.

Important Notice: Honey Loop Studio LLC is not responsible for delays caused by shipping carriers or customs processing.

Package Security

For your security, all packages require a signature upon delivery. If you're not available to receive your package, the carrier will leave a notice with instructions for redelivery or package pickup.
